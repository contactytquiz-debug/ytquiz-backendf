How it works

From YouTube link to ready‑to‑attempt quiz in three simple steps.

Paste a link, set your preferences, and YTQuiz handles the rest.

1
Paste your YouTube link

Copy a YouTube playlist or single video URL that contains the lessons or topics you want to create a quiz from, and paste it into the generator form below.

2
Configure your quiz

Choose how many questions you want per video, how many videos to include, and the difficulty level. YTQuiz uses these settings to control the length and depth of the quiz.

3
Generate & attempt the quiz

Click Generate quiz to go to your dedicated quiz page. YTQuiz creates concept‑focused questions from the content. Answer each question, submit the quiz, and instantly see your score and explanations.
